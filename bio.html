nesli

A native of Istanbul, Nesli seeks to discover the overlaps between the worlds of art, science and business in order to accommodate what our world needs at this very moment. As a life long learner, Nesli gets her inspiration from multi-faceted historical figures, such as René Descartes, Goethe, Leonardo Da Vinci, Johann Sebastian Bach, Hildegard von Bingen, Hypathia of Alexandria, Enheduanna of Sumer, having left their footprints in human history and consciousness. As our collective search for meaning continues so as Nesli’s personal quest for a boundless world.
